Contact

Only club members can access the chalets, and all chalet bookings must be from Friday to Sunday. If you would like to join as a club member, please email Club Maunga with the following information:

  • Full name
  • Physical address
  • Proof of address (e.g. copy of power bill)
  • Proof of ID (driver's licence or passport)

You can e-mail us by clicking the button below. Membership will be confirmed by email, and additional information about the Club and accommodation will be sent by regular mail via our memberships letter.
