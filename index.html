Welcome to Maunga Club!

Here at Maunga Club, we offer snow chalets and ski chalets to anyone interested in skiing or snowboarding down a mountain. All of our chalets are on the mountain, so anyone can ski from and to the chalets. The mountain has 550 metres of skiing/snowboarding area and the longest ski/snowboard season in all of New Zealand. If you are interested in staying at our chalets, please visit our contact page to found out more about how to join.
